Projects

Semester Research Projects

   A context-aware fine-grained skill extraction system using Natural Language Processing - Built a system that can classify skills into Soft Skills and Hard Skills and assign them to domains from job postings by using SOTA embeddings as linguistic features and building classification models on them.

   What is wrong with this product? - Built a system that can use reviews of products on social media to classify them as hazardous or non hazardous by training classification models on SOTA embeddings. This research work was awarded the EU product safety award by the European Commission and was published in the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Law (ICAIL 2023)

Internships

   Internship at Unilever,Rotterdam - This elective internship was performed at the Customer Development department of Unilever where I helped create a time series forecasting model to predict long term demand across channels (Lidl, Aldi etc.) in key cells (Netherlands, Germany etc). In addition to this, I also supported the Global Customer Development team with deliverables and helped harmonize data from various sources and created automated dashboards.

   Internship at Thirona Retina,Nijmegen - This thesis internship was performed at Thirona Retina where I implemented XAI techniques to explain the outputs of the Diabetic Retinopathy predicition and grading model. I also implemented quantitative evaluation techniques to measure the performance of these XAI techniques. My work on this thesis resulted in obtaining a grade of 8 in this thesis.
